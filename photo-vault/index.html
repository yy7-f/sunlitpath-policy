Privacy Policy

App: SecureGallery: Photo Vault

Last updated: January 2026

Introduction

SecureGallery: Photo Vault (the “App”) is designed with privacy as a core principle. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Photos and Media Access

The App requests access to your device’s photo library solely to allow you to select photos or videos to store inside your private vault.

  • All selected media is stored locally on your device only
  • No photos or videos are uploaded to any server or cloud
  • The developer has no access to your stored media
  • You can revoke photo access at any time via device settings

Camera Access

If enabled, the App may request camera access to allow you to take photos directly into the vault.

  • Captured photos remain on your device only
  • No camera data is transmitted or shared

Biometric & Device Security

The App may use biometric authentication (Face ID / Touch ID) or device passcodes to protect access to the vault.

  • Biometric data is handled entirely by your device’s operating system
  • The App does not store or access biometric data

Advertising (AdMob)

The App may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

  • AdMob may collect limited device information for ad delivery and analytics
  • No personal photos, videos, or vault content are shared with advertisers
  • Ad data handling is governed by Google’s privacy policies

Usage Data

Anonymous usage data (such as app stability or crash reports) may be collected to improve performance and reliability.

  • No personally identifiable information is collected
  • No vault content is included in analytics

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data. Data may be disclosed only if required by law.

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Parents or guardians should supervise device usage where appropriate.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
